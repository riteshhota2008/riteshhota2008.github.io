About Us

The team consists of 31 dedicated members who work hard to make the club successful. The Club is divided into 4 domains - Business, Creative, Public Relations and Technical.

The club has many future plans already in place. It plans to conduct another Applied CS course in the next academic year. Google Students Club conducts a week long fest 'Hangout' each year since its inception; which consists of various events and workshops. The workshops and events are conducted by the members of the club, with aid from authorized external personalities. Each year the fest witnesses a gigantic number of participation. We also conduct stand-alone workshops such as Design Sprint.

Google Students Club in SRM University aims to keep their good work intact while striving for further brilliance down the road.
