Budget Laptops for Students

Hear all the students! Your search for a multipurpose laptop, with minimum compromise and on a shoe-string budget, ends right here. Whether you're in school or college, here are our picks of the best laptops for students under 35k.

Learn More

#1 Dell Inspiron 15 3542



This 15-inch Dell Inspiron 15 comes with a 4th gen Intel Core i3 chip, with 4GB of RAM, and Ubuntu operating system. The laptop has 500GB HDD, and, Waves MaxxAudio 4 sound technology, which promises to give better audio performance than most other brands at this price point. All things considered, this is a good solid budget laptop to buy for students or general home use.

#2 Lenovo G50-70



Sporting 3rd generation Intel hardware, this ASUS laptop is definitely worth looking into. It has decent looks, and apart from the slower CPU, there's nothing to complain as it has a nice 15-inch form factor, 4GB RAM, 500GB HDD. At its budget, this one's definitely one of the best budget laptops for students, and well equipped for most of their needs.

#3 Acer Aspire E5-511



The Lenovo IdeaPad S210 Touch sports pretty similar specs to other 11-inch budget ultraportables in its class. However, its multi-touch enabled screen is a key feature on this ultraportable, which is easily one of the best budget laptops for students out there. A well-rounded machine for you to take to class or to study on the go.

#4 Hp Pavilion X360



Sporting an 11-inch screen, the HP Pavilion TouchSmart 11-e006AU sports an AMD chipset which is undoubtedly slower than a competing Intel chipset. However, its onboard graphics is definitely better than Intel's HD series (still not gaming worthy, though), which is very good trade-off. This is one of the best budget laptops for students, selling at a nice price, and definitely worth considering if you want to make an impressing with a good-looking notebook in class.

#5 Acer C720p Chromebook



The Acer C720P is a nice, cosy device -- slim, trim and easy to carry around. No complaints. In fact, it offers amazing battery life, best in class. The question you need to ask here is whether you're up to the challenge of leaving the cosy comfort of a Windows machine to try a unique new approach to a cloud operating system. It's like using a Chrome browser for everything. Buy this if you want to stand out in the class, and be different.

#6 HP 15-G002AX



This HP Chromebook is a larger version of the Acer C720p, and using it is an absolute joy. It's a looker, making heads turn, and is well designed and built. However, it is also hamstrung (or blessed) with the same Chrome OS which demands an always-on, always-connected user environment. Easily one of the best budget laptops for students, it's a unique machine, so you can definitely make heads turn at campus with this one.

#7 Asus X552EA-SX006D



One of the best budget laptops for students, the Sony VAIO Fit 15E is also a good looking specimen, as is the case with most VAIO laptops. This machine has a good hardware and feature list given the price that it sells at, assuring consumers of a basic usage experience that will go well with its charming looks. Can't go wrong with this one, students.

#8 Acer AspireE1-572



If you can find a copy of Windows operating system, then you need nothing else to get this machine up and running. Dressed in all-black, the Aspire E1-572 offers latest Intel hardware and better battery life from similarly priced competitors. It's head and shoulders above other laptops in this list, in terms of performance, if you have the budget for it. Hands down one of the best budget laptops for students this.

#9 Dell Inspiron 14 3442



For a 15-inch laptop, this HP Pavilion notebook weighs just over 2-kgs and has very good, almost premium look and design -- which is pretty unique in this price bracket. The laptop is great to work with and won't just take notes, but will also let you watch movies and songs, and many other things with ease. No gaming, though.
