Business Laptops

The definitive list to help you buy the top business laptops in India. For business of all kind, ranging from employees with a diverse range of job profile, we have filtered out the best business laptops, that which would appeal to the right audience, in this list. Dive in.

Learn More

#1 Hp 250 G3



If you're looking for a sturdy, well-rounded business laptop around Rs 35k, the HP 250 G3 is worth checking out. It has 15-inch LED backlit screen, 4th generation Intel Core i3 processor, and all the necessary input and connectivity features one would expect from a promising laptop. Weighing just over 2 kg, this can be taken out on sales calls or on field duty, too, if need be.

#2 Lenovo B50-70



A dependable business laptop this, the Lenovo B50-70, given its asking price of Rs 31k. It has good configuration: 4th gen Intel Core i3 chip, 4GB RAM, 1TB HDD, and AMD Radeon R5 M230 1GB GPU. Should be able to handle everything from large spreadsheets to power-packed rich media presentations at the work place.

#3 Toshiba Satellite Pro C40-B



The Satellite Pro C40 from Toshiba is another decent business laptop to consider at the budget end of the spectrum. It comes with latest Intel Core i3 chip, 4GB RAM, 500GB HDD, and AMD Radeon R5 M230 graphics. Doesn't come with Windows OS built-in, just in case your business establishment needs to load their own version of Windows on it. At the sub-35k price range, this Toshiba business laptop is well worth checking out.

#4 Dell Vostro 14 3446



A breath of fresh air, in the world of dull and boring looking business laptops, the Dell Vostro 14 3446 comes with Intel Core i5-4210U processor, 4GB RAM, and NVIDIA GeForce 820M graphics, which gives this laptop the ability to perform very well. Also, this particular model of the business laptop sells for Rs. 47k. Definitely worth buying if you need a business laptop with great performance.

#5 Hp Probook G40 G1



Selling at 55k, the HP ProBook 640 G1 is a great mid-ranged business laptop for not just employees but even mid-level executives. It's dressed formally, has all the latest hardware and features to offer good performance and connectivity options, and it comes with a trackpad for advanced navigation, and a dedicated fingerprint sensor. It has a built-in TPM chip for hardware level encryption, and a docking port, too. Good, well-rounded, true business laptop, if there was one at the price.

#6 Acer Travelmate P645



The Travelmate P645 is built well, which is a great quality to have in any business laptop, with no creaky joints or parts that flex overmuch - all credit to a metal alloy body and metal hinges. The 14-inch form factor of the laptop weighs just 1.52-kg which is great to carry it around the boardroom or long-distance flights. Its matte screen is great for reading texts and its non-reflective nature's a rarity on screens this day. The P645's security features include a fingerprint reader and onboard TPM module to encrypt all your sensitive data. One of the best business laptops out there.

#7 Hp EliteBook Folio 1040 G1



One of the best budget laptops for students, the Sony VAIO Fit 15E is also a good looking specimen, as is the case with most VAIO laptops. This machine has a good hardware and feature list given the price that it sells at, assuring consumers of a basic usage experience that will go well with its charming looks. Can't go wrong with this one, students.

#8 Toshiba Portege Z30t-A



Toshiba have really created quite a stellar device in the Portege Z30T-A. It's almost an ideal business laptop to have because it is very light, comfortable, portable, has great battery life, a great keyboard and a lovely screen. It's only let down by its very high asking price and somewhat bad touchpad. If you can afford it, this is the laptop you should be aiming for.

#9 Dell Precision M3800



Not just a business ultrabook this, it's a workstation ultrabook. With power-packed internal hardware, especially the workstation-class NVIDIA Quadro GPU, and other business-related bells and whistles. And it's only just over 2 cm thick and weighs a bit over 2 kg,despite all that firepower, the Precision M3800. This machine is definitely a looker! And worth buying if anyone wants a slim, trim, business laptop that can even take on some of the gaming laptop heavyweights with its performance potential.
